<p><p>The Oral Rehydration Salt (ORS) market comprises three main types: powder, tablet, and solution. Tablets offer a portable and convenient option for individual use, dissolving in water for quick rehydration. Solutions are pre-mixed fluids ready for consumption, providing an immediate alternative without preparation.
