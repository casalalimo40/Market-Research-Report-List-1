<p><p>Automated Tank Gauges (ATGs) are used to monitor the level and volume of liquids in storage tanks. The market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Float-operated Tank Gauges** use a buoyant float to measure liquid levels.</p><p>2. **Servo-operated Tank Gauges** employ a servo motor and gauge tape for precise measurement.</p><p>3. **Radar Tank Gauges** utilize radar signals to determine liquid levels with high accuracy.</p><p>4. **Hydrostatic Tank Gauges** measure pressure changes to calculate liquid height.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass emerging technologies and hybrid systems for specialized applications.</p></p>
