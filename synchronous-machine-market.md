<p><strong>The Synchronous Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Excitation Synchronous Motor</li><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synchronous machine market comprises two main types: Electric Excitation Synchronous Motors (EESM) and Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (PMSM). EESMs utilize external power sources for excitation, allowing for variable speed and higher efficiency in industrial applications. Conversely, PMSMs rely on embedded permanent magnets, offering compact designs, higher efficiency, and reliability, especially in applications like electric vehicles and robotics. Both types are crucial in enhancing energy efficiency and meeting evolving industrial demands.</p></p>
