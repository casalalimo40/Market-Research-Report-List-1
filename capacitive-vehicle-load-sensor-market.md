<p><p>The capacitive vehicle load sensor market comprises various types, including wheel spoke sensors, torque transducers, hole sensors, and S-sensors. Wheel spoke sensors measure load across the wheel, enhancing vehicle stability. Torque transducers assess engine torque for performance optimization. Hole sensors detect load variations in structural components, ensuring safety and durability. S-sensors integrate advanced technology to provide precise load measurements. Collectively, these sensors improve vehicle performance, enhance safety, and enable real-time monitoring, driving advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
