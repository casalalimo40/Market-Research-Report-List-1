<p><p>The fibrillated polypropylene fiber market is categorized into two main types: monofilament fiber and multifilament fiber. Monofilament fiber consists of single, continuous strands, offering strength and durability for applications like geotextiles and reinforcement. In contrast, multifilament fiber comprises multiple intertwined strands, providing flexibility and enhanced texture, making it suitable for various textiles and non-woven products. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, agriculture, and automotive, owing to their unique properties and versatility in application.</p></p>
