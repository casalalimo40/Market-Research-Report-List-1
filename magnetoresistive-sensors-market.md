<p><p>Magnetoresistive sensors are advanced devices that detect changes in magnetic fields, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. They work on the principle of magnetoresistance, where the electrical resistance of a material changes in response to an external magnetic field. These sensors are widely used in various applications, including automotive, industrial automation, consumer electronics, and healthcare, due to their robustness and reliability.</p><p>The Magnetoresistive Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.50% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for precise and efficient sensing technologies in various industries. As the Internet of Things (IoT) gains traction, the integration of magnetoresistive sensors in smart devices is becoming more prevalent. Additionally, advancements in sensor technology, such as the development of three-dimensional (3D) magnetoresistive sensors, are enhancing performance and expanding their application scope.</p><p>Key trends include the rising adoption of these sensors in automotive applications for electronic stability control and navigation systems, as well as their growing use in healthcare for monitoring vital signs. Furthermore, the push for automation and smart manufacturing is fueling the demand for magnetoresistive sensors, leading to continued market growth and innovation.</p></p>
