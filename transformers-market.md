<p><p>The transformers market consists primarily of power transformers and distribution transformers. Power transformers are used in high voltage applications for efficient transmission of electricity over long distances, ensuring minimal energy loss. In contrast, distribution transformers operate at lower voltages, stepping down electricity for safe delivery to residential and commercial consumers. Both types are essential for an effective electrical grid, facilitating energy flow from generation sources to end-users while ensuring reliability and safety in the distribution process.</p></p>
