<p><p>The Powder Injection Molding (PIM) Feed Market is divided into two primary segments: Metal Injection Molding (MIM) and Ceramic Injection Molding (CIM). MIM involves the processing of metal powders mixed with a thermoplastic binder, enabling the production of intricate metal parts with high precision and strength. In contrast, CIM focuses on ceramic materials, allowing for the creation of complex shapes and structures with excellent thermal and chemical stability. Both segments cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and medical applications.</p></p>
