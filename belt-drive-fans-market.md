<p><strong>The Belt Drive Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Type</li><li>Axial Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Belt Drive Fans market is segmented into two main types: Centrifugal and Axial fans. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to increase the air pressure and direct airflow radially, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and ducted systems. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan shaft, offering high flow rates and efficiency in low-pressure applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including HVAC, cooling systems, and process ventilation.</p></p>
