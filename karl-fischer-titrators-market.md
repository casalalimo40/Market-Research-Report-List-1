<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Titrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Karl Fischer titrators are essential analytical instruments for determining moisture content in various samples. The market is primarily divided into two types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration is ideal for measuring low moisture levels with high precision, using an electrochemical method to generate iodine. In contrast, volumetric titration is suitable for samples with higher moisture content, utilizing a reagent that directly reacts with water to quantify moisture levels. Both methods serve critical roles across industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals.</p></p>
