<p><p>Dry chemical fire extinguishers are categorized primarily into two types: portable and cart-mounted. Portable dry chemical extinguishers are small, easily maneuverable units designed for quick use in residential or small commercial settings. They typically have a limited capacity, making them ideal for immediate response. In contrast, cart-mounted fire extinguishers are larger, typically equipped with higher capacity and designed for industrial or larger commercial environments. These units are wheeled for easy transport and are suitable for tackling more significant fire risks.</p></p>
