<p><p>The Variable Speed Drive (VSD) market consists of three primary types: AC Drives, DC Drives, and Servo Drives. AC Drives control the speed and torque of AC motors, making them ideal for applications like fans and pumps. DC Drives manage direct current motors, offering variable speed control for industrial processes. Servo Drives are precision controllers that regulate the position, speed, and torque of servo motors, commonly used in robotics and automation. Each type addresses different needs in industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
