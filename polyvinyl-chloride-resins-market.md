<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins market is diverse, categorized by type, grade, and polymerization. Under type, there are rigid PVC resins, commonly used in construction and plumbing, and flexible PVC resins, utilized in wire insulation and flooring. By grade, PVC homopolymer resins are known for clarity and strength, while PVC copolymer resins offer improved flexibility. In terms of polymerization, suspension PVC resin dominates in rigid applications, bulk PVC resin is used for high-volume production, and emulsion PVC resin is preferred for coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
