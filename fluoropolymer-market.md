<p><p>The fluoropolymer market is typically categorized into solid and solution types. Solid fluoropolymers are used in applications requiring high thermal stability, chemical resistance, and durability, such as coatings, films, and gaskets. Solution fluoropolymers, on the other hand, are produced in liquid forms suitable for spray or dip coating processes and provide versatility in application methods.
