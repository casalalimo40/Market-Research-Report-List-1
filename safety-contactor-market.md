<p><p>The safety contactor market is primarily divided into two types: DC contactors and AC contactors. In contrast, AC contactors cater to alternating current applications, commonly found in industrial motors, lighting, and HVAC systems, ensuring reliable operation and automation. Both types are essential for enhancing safety and efficiency in electrical systems across various industries.</p></p>
