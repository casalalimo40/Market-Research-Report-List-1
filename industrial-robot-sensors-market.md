<p><p>The industrial robot sensors market encompasses various types, each serving distinct roles. Vision sensors enable robots to perceive and interpret their environments through imaging, essential for tasks like quality inspection. Force torque sensors measure the force and torque applied, ensuring precision in manipulation. Temperature sensors monitor the thermal conditions of equipment. Pressure sensors gauge system pressures, while position sensors track the location of robotic elements. Other sensors may include proximity and accelerometers, expanding the capabilities of robotic systems in industrial applications.</p></p>
