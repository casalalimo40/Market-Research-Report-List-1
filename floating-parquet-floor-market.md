<p><ul><li>Engineered</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating parquet floors are versatile flooring solutions that can be categorized into two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered parquet flooring consists of multiple layers, with a hardwood veneer on top, providing stability and resistance to humidity. Solid parquet flooring is made from a single piece of hardwood, offering durability and the ability to be refinished multiple times. Both types can be installed without nails or glue, allowing them to "float" over the subfloor, simplifying installation and maintenance.</p></p>
