<p><ul><li>Non-Cut-Off Luminaire</li><li>Semi-Cut-Off Luminaire</li><li>Full-Cut-Off Luminaire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road lighting fixtures market is categorized into three main types: Non-Cut-Off, Semi-Cut-Off, and Full-Cut-Off luminaires. Non-Cut-Off luminaires emit light in all directions, contributing to light pollution but providing widespread illumination. Semi-Cut-Off fixtures reduce upward light by directing more of it downward, improving visibility while minimizing glare. Full-Cut-Off luminaires are designed to emit light only downward, ensuring no light trespasses into the night sky, enhancing safety, energy efficiency, and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
