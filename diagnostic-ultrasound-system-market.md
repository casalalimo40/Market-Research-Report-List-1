<p><p>The Diagnostic Ultrasound System market is primarily segmented into Color Doppler and Black & White ultrasound imaging systems. Color Doppler ultrasound systems use advanced technology to visualize blood flow and vascular conditions, offering real-time color-coded images for improved diagnosis. In contrast, Black & White ultrasound imaging systems, also known as grayscale systems, provide detailed anatomical images but lack color differentiation. Both types serve crucial roles in medical imaging, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions through non-invasive techniques.</p></p>
