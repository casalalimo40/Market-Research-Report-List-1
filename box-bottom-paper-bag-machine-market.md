<p><p>The Box Bottom Paper Bag Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Sheet-Fed and Roll-Fed Paper Bag Machines. Sheet-Fed machines operate using pre-cut sheets of paper, enabling high precision and flexibility in designs, ideal for various bag styles. In contrast, Roll-Fed machines utilize continuous rolls of paper, allowing for faster production speeds and efficiency, making them suitable for high-volume output. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across industries, emphasizing sustainability and customization.</p></p>
