<p><p>The HVAC coils market primarily comprises evaporator coils and condenser coils. Evaporator coils are integral to cooling systems, absorbing heat from the air to facilitate temperature regulation in indoor spaces. In contrast, condenser coils release absorbed heat to the outside environment, completing the refrigeration cycle. Both types play crucial roles in energy efficiency and system performance, catering to residential, commercial, and industrial applications. The demand for these coils is driven by the growing need for effective climate control and energy conservation.</p></p>
