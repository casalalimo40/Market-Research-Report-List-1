<p><p>Programmable Delay Lines (PDLs) are critical components used to manage signal timing in various electronic applications. The market is segmented into Surface Mount Device (SMD) and Through Hole types. SMD PDLs are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of circuit boards, offering compactness and improved performance in high-density circuits. In contrast, Through Hole PDLs feature leads that pass through the board, providing robust connections and easier handling during assembly. Both types cater to different design requirements and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
