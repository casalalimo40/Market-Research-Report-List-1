<p><p>SLA Additive Manufacturing, or Stereolithography, is a 3D printing technology that utilizes a laser to cure liquid resin into solid parts layer by layer. Key trends include the development of biocompatible resins for medical applications, the integration of AI and automation in the printing process, and the emergence of eco-friendly resins to address sustainability concerns. As technological innovations continue to evolve, SLA additive manufacturing is anticipated to become more accessible, further expanding its application scope and market presence in the coming years.</p></p>
