<p><p>High shear granulators are essential in the pharmaceutical and food industries for the granulation process. The market can be categorized into three main types: dry powders, aqueous granulations, and solvent granulations. Dry powders involve the blending of powders to form granules without any liquid. Aqueous granulations utilize water as a binder, improving moisture content and flowability. Solvent granulations employ organic solvents to dissolve binding agents, enhancing granule formation and properties. Each type serves distinct needs based on product requirements and processing conditions.</p></p>
