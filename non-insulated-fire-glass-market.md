<p><p>The non-insulated fire glass market is categorized into two primary types: Monolithic Non-insulated Fire Glass and Composite Non-insulated Fire Glass. Monolithic fire glass consists of a single, solid piece, providing uniform strength and clarity, suitable for applications requiring high visibility and thermal resistance. Composite fire glass, on the other hand, consists of layered materials which enhance durability and impact resistance while allowing for design versatility. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and compliance in fire-rated building applications.</p></p>
