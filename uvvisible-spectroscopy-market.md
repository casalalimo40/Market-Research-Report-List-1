<p><strong>The UV/Visible Spectroscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-beam Systems</li><li>Dual-beam Systems</li><li>Array-based Systems</li><li>Handheld Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV/Visible spectroscopy market includes various system types catering to different analytical needs. Single-beam systems measure one spectrum at a time, ideal for simple applications. Dual-beam systems enhance accuracy by comparing a sample beam to a reference beam, minimizing environmental interferences. Array-based systems simultaneously capture multiple wavelengths, increasing throughput for high-volume analyses. Handheld systems offer portability and ease of use for on-site measurements, appealing to industries requiring quick, field-based analysis, such as environmental monitoring and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
